miRNA Tools for Alzheimer's Disease Research

Bioinformatics Tools & Databases

miRNA Databases

  • miRBase – Database of known miRNAs.
  • miRNet – Analyzes miRNA interactions.
  • miRDB – Functional miRNA target prediction.

miRNA Target Prediction Tools

  • TargetScan – Predicts miRNA target genes.
  • DIANA Tools – Analyzes miRNA regulatory networks.

miRNA Pathway & Network Analysis

  • KEGG Pathway Database – Identifies pathways regulated by miRNAs.
  • GeneMANIA – Predicts miRNA-gene interactions.
  • Cytoscape – Used for miRNA network visualization.

Expression Analysis & Biomarker Discovery

  • StarBase – Identifies miRNA-mRNA interactions in Alzheimer's disease.
  • GEO (Gene Expression Omnibus) – Provides Alzheimer’s miRNA expression datasets.

Clinical & Biomarker Research

  • ExoCarta – Database for exosomal miRNAs in Alzheimer's biomarker studies.
  • AlzData – Provides Alzheimer’s disease-related omics data, including miRNAs.
