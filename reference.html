    Alzheimer's Research Resources

    Resource Description Link
    Alzheimer's Disease Neuroimaging Initiative (ADNI) A longitudinal, multi-center observational study focused on validating biomarkers for Alzheimer's clinical trials. ADNI Website
    National Alzheimer's Coordinating Center (NACC) Maintains a comprehensive dataset from Alzheimer's Disease Research Centers, including clinical evaluations and neuropathology data. NACC Website
    International Alzheimer's and Related Dementias Research Portfolio (IADRP) Compiles global research investments and resources related to Alzheimer's and related dementias. IADRP Website
    NIAGADS (National Institute on Aging Genetics of Alzheimer's Disease Data Storage Site) Stores and distributes genetic and genomic data from Alzheimer's disease and aging studies. NIAGADS Website
    AlzForum Databases Offers interactive databases like AlzBiomarker, AlzGene, and AlzRisk, providing curated data on biomarkers, genetic associations, and risk factors. AlzForum Website
    Alzheimer's & Dementia Journal Provides open-access research papers on Alzheimer's disease and related disorders. Alzheimer's & Dementia Journal
